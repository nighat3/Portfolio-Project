A Passionate Data Science Student at Cornell.






Hi! My name is Nighat Ansari.
Welcome to my Website!

Born in Ahmedabad, India, I spent my childhood travelling between bustling Indian cities and boarding school. At thirteen, I moved to Massachusetts and have since become a Bostonian at heart.

From the day I moved to America, I dreamed of going to Cornell University. I knew nothing about Cornell or why it was prestigious, but knew only that "really smart" kids went there.

Today, as a rising junior at Cornell University, smart is the last word I would use to describe my peers. No one word can capture how persistent, accomplished, and passionate Cornell students are. Being a part of such a community has instilled in me goals that extend far beyond receiving certain grades or securing good internships.

Although I am only starting out in my career, I aspire to be a leader in technology someday. Leaps in technology are often synonymous with humankind's progress towards creating a more connected, equal, and prosperous world. To not only be a part of that legacy, but also help develop and lead new initiatives within it would enable me to affect positive change, no matter how small.

For now, I am focused on developing my technical and business background. My work experiences and coursework help me practically apply my skills in data-analytics, data science, software engineering, statistics, and marketing.
